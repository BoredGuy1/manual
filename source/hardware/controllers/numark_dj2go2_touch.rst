Numark DJ2GO2 Touch
===================

.. sectionauthor::
   tandy1000 <https://github.com/tandy-1000>

The Numark DJ2GO2 Touch is a USB :term:`MIDI` controller with an integrated audio interface.
It is the successor to the DJ2GO2, and features touch capable wheels.
